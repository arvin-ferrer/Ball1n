
# Ball1n

> **A high-stakes top-down shooter submitted for Game Jam+ 2025.** > *Theme: Small Scope, Big Fun*

You have a gun. You have **one** bullet. If you miss, you have to go get it.  
Defeat 11 bosses, level up, and master the art of the ricochet in this chaotic arena shooter.

---

## Controls

| Action | Key / Mouse | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move** | **`W` `A` `S` `D`** | Navigate the arena. |
| **Aim** | **`Mouse Cursor`** | Look around. |
| **Shoot** | **`Left Click`** | Fire your single bullet. Catch it to reload! |
| **Grenade** | **`G`** | Throw an explosive charge. |
| **Fastball** | **`F`** | Perform a high-speed dash/maneuver. |
| **Teleport** | **`R`** | Instantly teleport to your bullet's location and catch it. |

---

## Mechanics

### The Loop
1.  **Shoot:** Your bullet bounces off walls and pierces enemies.
2.  **Retrieve:** You cannot shoot again until you touch your bullet.
3.  **Survive:** Enemies will swarm you while you are unarmed.

### Skills
* **Teleport (R):** Shot your bullet into a dangerous corner? Press **R** to instantly warp to it, catch it, and reload immediately. Great for aggressive plays.
* **Grenade (G):** Crowd control. Throws a bomb that slows down and detonates after 2 seconds.
* **Fastball (F):** A quick burst of speed to dodge enemy attacks or chase your bullet.

### Progression
* **XP System:** Defeat enemies to gain XP.
* **Level Up:** Restore health and increase stats.
* **Objective:** Defeat all **11 Bosses** to win the game.
